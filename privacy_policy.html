Privacy Policy (English)

Our Flashcards App respects and protects your privacy.

  • We do not collect personal information unless you sign in with Google/Firebase.
  • Your learning data (flashcards, progress, preferences) is stored securely in Firebase and only accessible to you.
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to third parties.
  • You can request data deletion anytime by contacting us at: trantheo197@gmail.com
